Diet Recommendations During Your Period

Eating the right foods during your period can help reduce symptoms and improve your overall well-being. Focus on nutrient-rich foods that provide energy, reduce inflammation, and balance hormones.

1. Nutrient-Dense Foods

  • Iron-Rich Foods: Include spinach, lentils, red meat, and fortified cereals to replenish iron lost during menstruation.
  • Calcium and Vitamin D: Dairy products, almonds, and leafy greens support bone health and may ease cramps.
  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Found in fish like salmon and flaxseeds, they help reduce inflammation and mood swings.
  • Complex Carbohydrates: Whole grains, brown rice, and oats stabilize blood sugar levels and reduce cravings.

2. Foods to Limit or Avoid

  • Processed and Sugary Foods: These can increase bloating and worsen mood swings.
  • Excess Salt: May contribute to water retention and bloating.
  • Caffeine: Can increase anxiety and disrupt sleep patterns.
  • Alcohol: Might worsen PMS symptoms and interfere with sleep.

3. Hydration Tips

  • Drink Plenty of Water: Staying hydrated helps reduce bloating and flush toxins.
  • Herbal Teas: Chamomile, ginger, or peppermint teas can soothe cramps and relax the body.
